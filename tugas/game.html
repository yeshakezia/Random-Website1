Games Recommendation

Don't know what to play? These are some great games you can play in your free time.

Growtopia Avakin Life The Sims 4 GTA V Mobile Legends PUBG Clash Of Clan Valorant
Dark Mode

Growtopia

This game is one of the games that was popular in 2016-2020. This game is a place where you can meet people from various countries and do business. In addition, in this game you can also make various types of objects that you can sell at high prices here.

Avakin Life

This game is a virtual world game where you will be asked to create your own character there and can buy a house or apartment for your avatar character. In addition, you can also work and meet people from all over the country to play together. This game is perfect for those of you who don't really like fighting games because this game is specifically designed for healing games.

THE SIMS 4

The Sims game is basically not an online multiplayer game that allows you to meet friends from all over the world directly in one game world (it used to be possible but was discontinued in 2008). This game is a game in which you can create your own family and house. However, if you want to play with friends, people usually use the mod version of this game even though it is not official. However, that also applies if you and your friends have the same server and internet connection.

GTA V

Similar to the Avakin Life game, this game is also a virtual world game but this game is equipped with several types of games, for example there is a zombie apocalypse. In addition, in this game there is a survival mode. This game is a game that is quite popular until now because it has a story chain that can be enjoyed by players, only this game is a paid game.

Mobile Legends

If you want a more challenging and adrenaline-pumping game, you can play this game. This game is a fighting game that you can play with your friends. Here you will form a team of 5 people then you will work together to defeat the opponent's base.

PUBG

Similar to the mobile legend game, this is also an adrenaline-pumping game. Here you are assigned to be a reliable shooter who must defeat all enemies. In this game you can choose various types of games, there are team games, duos and solos. Your shooting skills and accuracy are really tested in this game. In addition, you can also play it with your friends who are all over the country.

Clash Of Clan

Quite different from other fighting games, this game focuses more on defending our own village from enemy attacks. In addition, we can also upgrade our troops to be able to destroy enemy villages. This game can also be played with friends by joining the same clan. In a clan, all members will sometimes get special missions to fight certain clans.

Valorant

This game is not much different from the pubg game, which is a shooting game. It's just that, in this game 1 team has 5 members who have the task of eliminating enemies in the game.
